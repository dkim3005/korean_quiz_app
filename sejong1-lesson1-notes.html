Sejong Korean 1

Lesson 1: 자기소개 (Self-introduction)

Learning Objectives

  • Introduce your name and nationality.
  • Ask for someone's name and nationality.
  • Use the topic marking particles 은/는.
  • Use the verb 'to be' in the polite form, 이에요/예요.
